# Security acknowledgements

This page lists external security researchers whose reports led to a valid
finding of **Medium severity or above** and who consented to public credit
under the Loop bug-bounty program.

See [bug-bounty.md](./bug-bounty.md) for scope, rewards, and how to submit.

## Hall of fame

_No public acknowledgements yet — the program is in launch phase. Researchers
whose reports are accepted will be listed here with their consent._

## How to be listed

When you submit a report through HackerOne, you can opt in to public credit.
On acceptance we will add your handle (and optionally your link of choice)
to the list above on the next docs deploy.
